Welcome to Wanna Call

WannaCall is innovation in its simplest form, connecting people across the world and creating passive income streams. We are a mobile application that is designed to allow you to connect with people and access services over a quick and easy phone call. We use a pay-per-min VoIP connection so that you pay for the time you spend interacting.
